Making a Graffiti App Prototype:

One of my first classes I took in College was interactive media design. Which was a class all about learning the design process and how to effectively do each step. For one of the big assignments we were tasked with creating a prototype for an app all about making a graffiti social media platform. I learned how to wireframe my designs while also learning about practicality and design aesthetics.
